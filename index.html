CLUB MECH

EST. 1937

    Welcome!

    We are Club MECH, UBC's mechanical engineering undergraduate student club. Among other things, we run social and networking events, and act as representatives for mechanical engineering undergrads. Our clubroom is located in CEME 2207 – every MECH student has access!

    Our meetings are every Wednesday at 7:00 PM in the clubroom, and are open to all MECH students. Want to get involved? Check out the open positions, and if something interests you, reach out!
